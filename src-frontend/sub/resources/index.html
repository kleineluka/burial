Resources ✂️ Decryption 🔪 Encryption 🩹 Sift 🫧 Templates 🥺 Dialogue Parser 📖 Save Editor ✏️

The tools in Resources focus on extracting assets.

🔪 Decryption restores .k9a files to their original format.

🩹 Encryption encrypts files to .k9a format.

🫧 Sift sorts and exports specific categories of assets.

🥺 Templates creates sprite templates from game assets.

📖 Dialogue Parser exports and imports text from the game.

✏️ Save Editor decrypts, modifies, and manages your save files.
